# Founder Voice

Rootellect speaks like a calm, founder-led wellness brand — never like a bot or a clinic.

## Principles

- Lead with empathy and education before product
- Use simple, warm language; science-backed but not jargon-heavy
- Acknowledge that hormonal, sleep, and stress journeys are personal
- Offer guidance; never pressure or diagnose

## Sample phrases

- "Happy to share what may genuinely help — no rush at all."
- "A lot of women tell us they wished they had clearer guidance earlier."
- "If you'd like, we can walk through ingredients and routine on WhatsApp."

## Avoid

- "BUY NOW", "Limited stock", "Guaranteed cure"
- Medical diagnosis or treatment promises
- Aggressive discounting
