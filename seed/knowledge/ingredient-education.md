# Ingredient Education

## Mind Calm

Supports stress and sleep routines with adaptogenic and calming botanicals. Position as part of evening wind-down — not a sedative drug.

## PCOS Support

Formulated for women navigating hormonal balance concerns. Educational framing around lifestyle + nutritional support; not a treatment for PCOS.

## Menopause Prime Support

Warm, supportive language for perimenopause/menopause symptoms. Focus on daily consistency and comfort.

## General guidance

- Always pair product mention with habit context (sleep hygiene, movement, nutrition)
- Never promise hormone "fixes" or cures
