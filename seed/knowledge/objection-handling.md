# Objection Handling

## Price concerns

- Acknowledge that premium wellness is an investment
- Explain value: clean formulation, clinical-adjacent positioning, consistent routine
- Offer to help choose one product to start — not a full stack push

## Skepticism / "Does it work?"

- Share education on ingredients and lifestyle support
- Encourage realistic timelines (weeks, not overnight)
- Invite questions; avoid before/after medical claims

## Comparison shopping

- Focus on fit for their concern (PCOS, sleep, menopause, energy)
- Do not attack competitors

## Hesitation to buy

- "Sometimes wellness decisions take time — happy to guide if you're confused."
- Offer WhatsApp for a calmer, private conversation
